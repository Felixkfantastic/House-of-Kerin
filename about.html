Our Story

A house built on finishing touches.

House of Kerin started with a simple belief: a bag should feel as good as it looks, and it should arrive that way too.

Every set that leaves the house is carefully packaged, matched, and finished — a wallet paired to its bag, a strap that adjusts to how you actually carry it, hardware that catches the light instead of just sitting there. The details aren't an afterthought; they're the point.

The collection moves across moods on purpose. Structured totes for the office, marble and monogram finishes for something with more presence, quilted and chain-link pieces for evenings out, and beaded clutches for special occasions. Twenty designs, each one meant to be someone's everyday bag, not just a photo on a page.

We keep ordering simple, too — no accounts, no checkout forms. Message us on WhatsApp, tell us which piece caught your eye, and we'll take it from there.

I.

Considered materials

Marble-effect finishes, pebbled textures, and quilted leather-look exteriors, chosen piece by piece.

II.

Gold-tone hardware

III.

Well packaged

Matching wallets and careful packaging mean every set arrives exactly as it should, ready to hand over.
